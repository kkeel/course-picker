1) Choose courses & topics

Browse by subject and grade, then bookmark the courses and topics you plan to use. Turn on My Courses to focus on your selections.

  • Use filters to narrow by grade, subject, and planning tags.
  • Add planning tags to help you track term plans, co-op plans, or student assignments.
  • Use “My Notes” to keep your personal planning thoughts right alongside the curriculum.

2) Build your Book List

Your Book List reflects the resources connected to your selected courses/topics. Bookmark books as you gather them, and track preparation status if needed.

  • Turn on My Books to see only what you plan to use.
  • Use preparation tracking (digital/physical, ordered/received/ready) to stay organized.
  • Print when you’re ready—covers and page numbers are handled for you.

3) Print & share

Both lists are designed to work on screen and on paper. Use print to create a clean, shareable copy for your home, co-op, or school.

Printing works best in Chrome
If covers look slow to load, give the print window a moment to finish “preparing.”
