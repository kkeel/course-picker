Core Subjects
Focus on your must-have requirements (e.g., Bible, History, Geography, English/Reading, Literature, Science, Math). Note any stream changes (e.g., only Ancient), or planned combinations (e.g., G2+G3 in Science).
High-Interest Subjects
Pick a few spark subjects per child or for the family. Rotate by term if needed (e.g., Shakespeare ➝ Plutarch ➝ Composer) or stretch a term over two for a gentler pace.
Additional Subjects
Add one or two priorities you haven’t covered yet—credits, family values, or future goals. These can include non-Alveary resources (co-ops, ACT prep) or skills like cooking/car care.
Plan Your Year
Build your timetable with Alveary’s tools. Keep a short “maybe” list to add later. See “Relational Education,” “Balancing the Feast,” and “Tips for Combining Students” for guidance.
